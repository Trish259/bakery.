About Sweet Treats Bakery

Founded in 2010, Sweet Treats Bakery has been delighting customers with our selection of freshly baked pastries, cakes, and custom desserts. What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into a beloved community staple, known for our top-quality ingredients and delectable treats.


Our Mission

At Sweet Treats Bakery, our mission is simple: to create joy through food. We are committed to using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients in all our baked goods, ensuring that every bite delivers comfort and happiness. Our goal is to provide a warm and inviting experience for all our customers, whether you're stopping by for a morning pastry or ordering a custom cake for a special occasion.


Our Values

We believe in the power of community, creativity, and sustainability. That's why we work closely with local farmers and suppliers to bring the freshest ingredients into our bakery. We are also dedicated to reducing waste and minimizing our environmental impact by using eco-friendly packaging and supporting sustainable practices in our daily operations.


Meet the Team

Our bakery wouldn't be what it is today without the passion and dedication of our incredible team. From the bakers to the customer service staff, everyone plays a crucial role in delivering an unforgettable experience to our customers.


Emily Thompson

Founder & Head Baker

With over 15 years of experience in the baking industry, Emily founded Sweet Treats Bakery with the vision of bringing people together through the joy of delicious baked goods. Her expertise in pastry and cake design has made her a local favorite, and her commitment to quality is evident in every creation that leaves the bakery.
